ZLN is a renewable energy management and group services company that provides essential and holistic solutions to the retail, commercial, industrial, and residential sectors. We are committed to helping clients reduce their carbon emissions in Scope 1, 2, and 3.

Next Generation Energy Solutions

Residential Grid Tie

Homeowners can utilize the electricity generated by their solar panels both for their own use and to send excess energy back to the grid for credit or compensation.

Large Scale Utility Installations

We specialize in designing and installing MegaWatt projects for utility grid-feed infrastructure facilities that cater to the essential needs of communitties and industries.

Diesel Hybrid PV Systems

A diesel hybrid PV system is a combination of diesel generators and photovoltaic panels that generates electricity efficiently using both renewable and non-renewable energy sources, reducing fuel consumption and emissions from the diesel generator.

Commercial renewable energy solutions

Our aim to reduce a business's carbon footprint, lower energy costs, and provide a more reliable and secure source of energy for businesses and commercial buildings.

Residential PV Systems

Solar energy systems for homes and small buildings consist of solar panels, an inverter, and a monitoring system.

Large Scale Battery Storage Systems

Large scale battery storage systems can be used in conjunction with a photovoltaic (PV) system to provide continuous power supply. This makes it a useful solution for various types of sites including retail, agriculture, commercial, and industrial.
